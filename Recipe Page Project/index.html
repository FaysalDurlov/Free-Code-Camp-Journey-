Creamy Chocolate Fudge

Description

Creamy Chocolate Fudge is a classic homemade treat that boasts a rich, velvety texture and an indulgent chocolate flavor. Made with simple ingredients like melted chocolate , butter, and condensed milk, this fudge is smooth, sweet, and melts in your mouth wit h every bite. Whether enjoyed as a snack or a dessert, its luscious consistency and de ep cocoa taste make it a favorite for chocolate lovers of all ages.

Perfect for holidays, special occasions, or gifting, this fudge is easy to prepare and requires minimal effort. It can be customized with nuts, caramel swirls, or a sprinkle of sea salt for added depth and contrast. Whether served as bite-sized squares or packaged beautifully for friends and family, creamy chocolate fudge is a timeless delight that brings comfort and joy with each piece.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (12 oz) semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • ¼ cup (½ stick) unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Prepare the Pan
  2. Melt the Ingredients
  3. Add Flavor
  4. Pour and Set
  5. Chill and Cut
